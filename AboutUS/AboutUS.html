About Adamant Immigraion Consulting

“Adamant” Immigration Consulting (AIC) was founded in Toronto, GTA to serve clients all over the world to solve their urgent immigration needs. We are registered members in good standing with the Immigration Consultants of Canada Regulatory Council (RCIC #R527869) who have the required knowledge and experience to help you successfully immigrate to Canada and make the process as smooth and hassle free as possible.​

Our consultants will guide you through dozens of immigration programs, streams and classes and will find for you the most convenient option to make your Canadian dream come true. We truly love what we do and welcome the opportunity to assist you with your immigration needs
