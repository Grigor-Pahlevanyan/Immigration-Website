Temporary Resident Visas/Business Visas to Canada

Persons who are not Canadian citizens or permanent residents of Canada may require a visa to enter Canada. Depending on the purpose of visit they may require either tourist or business visa. Business visitors will need an invitation from Canadian organization that will indicate the reason of the visit. In addition, business visitors must demonstrate that they do not plan to enter the Canadian labour market and their main source of income and profits is outside Canada.â€‹

If you do not require a visa to enter Canada, you may require an Electronic Travel Authorization (eTA).
